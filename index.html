Our Story

Crafted in Freetown,
Worn by the World.

Salone Scents was founded in Freetown with one belief: Sierra Leone deserves a luxury fragrance brand that reflects its own beauty. Our perfumes are inspired by the places, plants, and people of Sierra Leone.

Every bottle tells a story — Lumley Beach at dawn, the cotton tree in the heart of Freetown, the wild jasmine of the hills above Regent. We source botanicals locally where possible, supporting Sierra Leonean farmers and artisans.

  • Inspired by Sierra Leone's natural landscapes and culture
  • Locally sourced botanicals supporting SL farmers
  • Premium ingredients, crafted for the African climate
  • Affordable luxury — made in Salone, for everyone
  • Available nationwide and for international shipping

Made in Sierra Leone

Natural Ingredients

We source local botanicals directly from Sierra Leonean farmers — supporting communities while creating authentic, natural scents.

💎

Affordable Luxury

Premium fragrance doesn't have to be imported. Salone Scents delivers luxury quality at prices that are accessible across Sierra Leone.
