# perfume FC

## About
perfume is a football club dedicated to promoting talent, teamwork, and excellence in the sport. The club focuses on developing players’ skills both on and off the field while encouraging discipline, unity, and sportsmanship.

## Mission
To nurture young football talents and provide opportunities for growth, competition, and success through structured training and teamwork.

## Vision
To become a leading football club known for producing skilled players and contributing positively to the football community.

## Core Values
- Teamwork
- Discipline
- Respect
- Hard work
- Excellence

## Activities
- Regular training sessions
- Friendly matches and competitions
- Talent development programs
- Community engagement through sports
